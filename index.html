Inspiration

Saul Bass

Saul Bass has been a constant source of inspiration in my career as a graphic designer. His ability to transform complex concepts into simple and elegant designs, as seen in the titles of 'Psycho' and 'Vertigo,' demonstrates that less can be more. Bass captured the essence of cinematic narrative and corporate identity with a distinctive and powerful style. His logos and posters were not only visually striking but also told stories. His innovative approach has taught me to value simplicity and clarity in design, inspiring me to create with purpose and elegance.

Rene Gruau

Rene Gruau has been a fundamental inspiration in my approach to graphic design. His ability to capture the elegance and dynamism of fashion with precise lines and bold compositions has profoundly influenced my work. Gruau's figurines, with their distinctive and sophisticated style, have fueled my passion for fashion and shown me how graphic design can be an extension of fashion. His capacity to merge art and style in each illustration has motivated me to explore new ways to express my love for fashion through graphic design.

Maurice Sendak

Another artist who has been a source of inspiration is Maurice Sendak. His work Where the Wild Things Are has left a lasting impression on me, demonstrating how illustrations can capture the imagination and address deep themes. Sendak's skill in using vibrant colors and evocative narratives has influenced my graphic design work. His approach has motivated me to create designs that are not only visually appealing but also emotionally engaging, much like his beloved children's illustrations.
