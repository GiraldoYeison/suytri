Biomedicas

In my role at Biomedicas, a chemical company specializing in innovative products, I had the privilege of leading the graphic design for several key projects. One of the standout tasks was creating the booth for a major exhibition where various brands showcased their offerings.

I was responsible for designing and coordinating all the graphic elements for the event, ensuring that our presence was impactful and professional. Every detail of the booth reflected our vision and quality, helping us stand out among competitors and capture the attention of attendees.

VI International Symposium

At the first International Research Symposium of the Universidad Andina, Pereira section, I had the honor of being responsible for designing all the graphic materials for the event. From conceptualization to execution, my goal was to ensure a flawless and cohesive presentation.

Additionally, I managed the overall organization and coordination of the symposium, overseeing the exhibitors and ensuring the schedule was adhered to smoothly. Every graphic element and every detail of the event reflect my commitment to excellence and my passion for design.

Bosques del Saman hotel

As a graphic designer, I had the privilege of designing all the signage for the Bosques del Saman hotel. This project involved creating over 50 graphic pieces, ranging from signage for each of the hotel's fruit trees to welcome cards in the rooms. Each design was carefully crafted to enhance the guest experience and reflect the essence of the hotel. My role included both the conceptualization and design of each piece, as well as overseeing their printing and installation, ensuring every detail was flawlessly executed.

Anana

I later pursued a certificate in Brand Architecture. During this semester-long specialization, I worked in a team of three, including two colleagues and myself. Our project focused on creating the brand Anana, covering all aspects of branding, from logo design to catalog creation and local model campaigns.

We targeted the children's niche, incorporating creative designs inspired by an indigenous culture, highlighting elements such as fishing and the tribe's diet. The result was a vibrant and emotional brand, with clothing made from soft fabrics that convey joy and cultural connection for young children.

ISAGEN

One of the projects I worked on was designing sports uniforms for ISAGEN, a company dedicated to renewable energy generation and commercialization. As part of our commitment to the community, we also support youth sports. I had the privilege of designing a complete range of sports uniforms, including basketball, kayaking, futsal, tennis, track and field, and volleyball for both men and women. Each uniform was crafted with a focus on functionality and style, reflecting ISAGEN's commitment to sports and youth.
